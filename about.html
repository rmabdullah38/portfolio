Who I Am

I'm Ronny Abdullah, a passionate Electrical Engineering student at Stanford University with a concentration in Artificial Intelligence. I'm currently maintaining a 3.7 GPA while pursuing my Bachelor's degree, expected to graduate in June 2026.

My academic journey has been marked by several honors and fellowships, including being a Rising Bird Fellow, Dalai Lama Fellow, and receiving the Award of Excellence. I'm also proud to be an SSFP Scholar and SSEA Scholar, recognizing my commitment to academic excellence and research innovation.

I'm deeply involved in research at Stanford, working in the Robust Systems Group under Professor Subhasish Mitra on AI for hardware verification. This research combines my passion for both hardware systems and artificial intelligence, allowing me to work on cutting-edge projects that bridge these two domains.
